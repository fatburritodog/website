Imminent Loss: Film Festival

Branding, Concept, Photography

A film festival that features the works of David Fincer and Steve McQueen. I was very interested in the films that these directors make because they are quite dark and twisted. The films are very complex and deal with very heavy topics. Characters in their films also have very intricate and delicate relationships with one another. While watching their films, I felt that loss was one of the main reoccuring themes of their works. 

The main motif: a moth
